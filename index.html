A Little About Me

Graduated from San Francisco State University on December 2017 with a B.S. Degree in Computer Science

Born in India, raised in Singapore, and blossomed in America, I am incredibly privileged to have a diverse ethnic background. Moving to America as a ten year old, I was often confused if I be excited to visit Disneyland, or worried that I have to move away from my childhood friends. Although uncertain then, I am more than grateful to have obtained this experience as it has helped me grow into a more confident individual, learn to quickly adapt to new environments, and be prepared to face future hurdles with a positive outlook on life.

Being a big coffee fanatic and connoisseur of food, I often times find myself lost in cities in the search of cafes and restaurants. During my spare time, I enjoy adventuring around, cooking/baking dishes from different ethnicities, and drawing pencil portraits.
